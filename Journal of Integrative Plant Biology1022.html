Journal of Integrative Plant Biology (JIPB) is a peer-reviewed monthly journal that reports the latest plant biology discoveries. It was established in 1952 as Acta Botanica Sinica, and is sponsored by the Botanical Society of China and the Institute of Botany, Chinese Academy of Sciences. The journal publishes 6 types of articles: breakthrough reports, commentaries, invited review articles, new resources, new technologies, and full-length research articles. JIPB’s 5-year SCI impact factor (IF) is 4.061. The following are the latest issue.
