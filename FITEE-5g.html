Frontiers of Information Technology & Electronic Engineering (FITEE) is an international peer-reviewed journal launched by Chinese Academy of Engineering (CAE) and Zhejiang University, co-published by Springer & Zhejiang University Press. Being one of the transactions of CAE (since 2015), FITEE is aimed to publish the latest implementation of applications, principles, and algorithms in the broad area of Electrical and Electronic Engineering. FITEE is indexed by SCI-E (IF=1.033), Ei Compendex, DBLP, Scopus, etc.
    Recently FITEE has published a special feature on 5G wireless communication systems and technologies (Guest Editors: Prof. Ping ZHANG, BUPT). Below are 9 selected papers from this issue. We hope you enjoy reading them.
